title: LongShadowTextBlock
---

# Attributes
|Property|
|-|
|TextBlock|
|Color|
|Depth|
|TextOpacity|

# Example

```xml
<wuc:LongShadowTextBlock Depth="100" TextOpacity="0.3" Color="GreenYellow">
    <wuc:LongShadowTextBlock.TextBlock>
        <TextBlock FontSize="100" Text="Text" />
    </wuc:LongShadowTextBlock.TextBlock>
</wuc:LongShadowTextBlock>
```
